Privacy Policy

How AI Impact Maine collects, uses, and protects your information.

Scroll
Last updated: May 2026

AI Impact Maine ("we," "our," or "us") is an LLC based in Portland, Maine. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website at aiimpactmaine.com and interact with our services.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you contact us, request a resource, book a discovery call, use the client portal, or complete our free assessment, you may provide:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and organization name
  • Information about your organization's AI use and challenges
  • Messages, client portal notes, survey responses, invoices, contracts, and uploaded or downloaded materials connected to services

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our site may collect standard technical information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, approximate geographic region, and security logs used to protect the website and client portal.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule discovery calls
  • Deliver services you've requested (training, audits, advisory support)
  • Send occasional updates about our services or the Maine AI Impact Conference — you can opt out at any time
  • Improve our website and service offerings

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain contact and inquiry information for as long as needed to fulfill the purpose it was collected for, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your information at any time by emailing hello@aiimpactmaine.com.

Service Providers

We use service providers to operate the website and deliver client services. These may include Cloudflare for hosting, security, D1 database, R2 file storage, and email routing; Resend for email delivery; Stripe for payment links and payment processing; Google services for fonts, maps, and workspace tools; and Formspree for legacy or fallback form handling if needed.

These providers process information only as needed to provide their services. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies required for admin access, client portal sessions, security, and basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies. If this changes, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notice.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@aiimpactmaine.com.

Security

We use HTTPS, limited-access admin tools, short-lived client portal sessions, hashed login tokens, and access controls around client materials. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. Please avoid sending highly sensitive information unless it is necessary for the service.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at hello@aiimpactmaine.com or through our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our services evolve. Material changes will be noted by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

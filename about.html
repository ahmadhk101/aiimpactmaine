Our Story

Built for Maine. Focused on what actually works.

AI Impact Maine was founded to fill a real gap: most Maine businesses are already using AI — through tools like ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Grammarly, and dozens of others — but few have the training, governance frameworks, or ongoing support to use it safely and strategically.

We bring practical business experience alongside a genuine commitment to responsible AI adoption. Our work is designed for real organizations — not just tech-forward companies, but construction firms, nonprofits, municipalities, professional services practices, and any organization navigating AI in its daily operations.

We speak plain English. We focus on outcomes. We stay focused on what matters most: helping your team understand AI well enough to use it responsibly — and benefit from it meaningfully.

As an organization rooted in Southern Maine, we care about the long-term health of the Maine business community. We're structured as an LLC, giving us the flexibility to grow sustainably while staying focused on the work that matters to the organizations we serve.

Why an LLC?

Operating as a Limited Liability Company keeps AI Impact Maine simple, sustainable, and accountable. It lets us deliver real, paid business services to Maine organizations while keeping decision-making lean and our mission firmly in our own hands.

We are based in Portland, Maine and serve businesses throughout Southern Maine and statewide — onsite where it matters and virtually wherever needed.

How We Work

The principles that shape every engagement.

01

Education First

Every service we offer starts from a commitment to genuine education — helping your team understand AI deeply enough to make good decisions, not just follow rules.

02

Practical Over Perfect

We don't wait for the perfect policy or the ideal moment. We help you build something real and workable now — and improve it over time as your organization grows into it.

03

Responsible AI, Not AI Hype

We don't evangelize AI. We help organizations use it thoughtfully — with clear eyes about the risks, honest assessment of the benefits, and policies that reflect both.

04

Local Long-Term Impact

We're here because we care about Maine's business community. Every engagement is designed with long-term value in mind — not just a deliverable to close a contract.

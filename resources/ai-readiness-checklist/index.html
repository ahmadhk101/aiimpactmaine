Maine Business AI Readiness Checklist

50-question AI readiness assessment for Maine organizations covering strategy, technology, skills, data, and governance — with maturity scoring and a personalized implementation roadmap.

📄 22-page guide ⏱️ 30 minute read ✓ Ready to implement

What’s inside

Find out exactly where your organization stands on AI before you invest a dollar. 50 questions across five dimensions, a scoring matrix that maps to maturity levels, and a personalized roadmap based on your gaps.

  • 50 assessment questions across strategy, technology, skills, data, and culture
  • Scoring matrix with five maturity levels (Aware → Optimizing)
  • Gap analysis worksheet
  • Sector benchmarking data (where peer Maine orgs typically score)
  • Personalized 90-day, 6-month, and 12-month roadmap templates

Who should use this

Anyone leading or scoping an AI initiative who needs evidence — not opinion — about organizational readiness.

  • Executive directors and COOs evaluating AI investment
  • IT leads building a business case for AI tooling
  • Consultants and grant writers documenting client readiness

Key outcomes

After using this resource, you’ll be able to:

  • Replace gut feel with a numbered, defensible readiness score
  • Identify the three highest-leverage gaps to close first
  • Set a realistic 12-month AI roadmap your board can approve
  • Benchmark against similar Maine organizations

How to use

Print the checklist and complete it with two or three colleagues from different functions. Compare scores — divergences reveal blind spots. Use the gap analysis worksheet to prioritize.

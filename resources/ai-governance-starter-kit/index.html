AI Governance Starter Kit

Complete AI governance toolkit with frameworks, policies, RACI matrices, approval workflows, committee structures, and meeting templates for organizations of any size.

📄 45-page guide ⏱️ 40 minute read ✓ Ready to implement

What’s inside

Everything you need to stand up an AI governance function in a small or mid-size organization. Frameworks, policy standards, decision-making matrices, committee structures, and the routine meeting documents that keep governance from drifting.

  • Governance framework with operational, tactical, and strategic levels
  • Roles and responsibilities matrix (RACI) for AI decisions
  • Five core policy standards: tool approval, data handling, transparency, ethics, compliance
  • Six-step tool evaluation process and approval workflow
  • Monthly meeting agenda, quarterly review template, annual strategy outline
  • Sample committee charter and decision log

Who should use this

Organizations ready to operationalize AI governance — typically after a first incident, board ask, or audit finding.

  • Mid-size businesses, nonprofits, and municipalities
  • Compliance and risk leaders building an AI program
  • Boards or audit committees standing up an AI oversight function

Key outcomes

After using this resource, you’ll be able to:

  • Stand up an AI governance committee with documented authority
  • Replace ad-hoc AI decisions with a repeatable approval workflow
  • Document AI risk and oversight for auditors, funders, or regulators
  • Reduce time-to-decision on new AI tool requests

How to use

Customize the committee charter and approval workflow to your existing governance structure. Adopt the meeting templates as-is — the routine is more important than the format. Review quarterly.

FAQ

Payment and customization questions.

Every tier is designed as a starting point. Final scope can be adjusted before payment when your organization needs something more specific.

Can pricing be customized?

Yes. The listed ranges are packaged starting points. Enterprise work, grant-funded engagements, and multi-service bundles can be scoped after a consultation.

How do refunds work?

For one-time projects, refunds depend on scheduling and work already completed. Subscription retainers can be cancelled or changed at renewal unless a separate agreement says otherwise.

Do we need to pay before talking?

No. You can schedule a consultation first. Checkout links are best for organizations that already know the tier they want to start with.

Not sure which package fits?

Book a consultation and we will recommend a tier, bundle, or custom scope based on your goals.

Why This Matters

AI readiness shouldn't only be for well-resourced organizations.

Many Maine businesses — nonprofits, small trades firms, municipalities, community organizations — are already using AI tools without the policy, governance, or training to do so safely. They're also the least likely to have the budget to address it.

Grant and sponsorship support allows AI Impact Maine to serve these organizations at reduced or no cost — while sustaining the organization through market-rate engagements with larger businesses.

Mission

Responsible AI education and adoption for Maine organizations, with special focus on underserved sectors.

Model

low-profit LLC structure balances grant eligibility with earned revenue — sustainable by design, not dependent on any single funder.

Geography

Deep focus on Southern Maine and statewide reach — delivered in-person where it matters and virtually where needed.

Ways to Partner

Multiple entry points for mission-aligned partners.

Program Underwriting

Fund AI training workshops or audit services for specific organizations or sectors — your support directly enables responsible AI adoption.

Named Sponsorship

Associate your organization with Maine's emerging responsible AI ecosystem through event and workshop sponsorships with community visibility.

Pilot Co-Design

Partner with us to design and deliver a new training model, audit framework, or resource guide for a specific Maine sector or audience.

Not sure what fits? We're happy to have a conversation about what partnership could look like for your foundation, agency, or organization.

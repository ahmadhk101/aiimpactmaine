Maine Workforce Development Compact

The Compact may provide matching workforce training funds for eligible Maine employers, including up to $1,200 per frontline employee for approved training.

Useful for

  • AI literacy and responsible-use training
  • Prompting and productivity workshops
  • AI readiness and workflow improvement sessions
  • Governance, policy, and risk training

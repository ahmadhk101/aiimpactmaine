Why Now

AI is already in your workplace. Is your organization ready?

Most businesses are already using AI tools — ChatGPT, Copilot, Grammarly — without formal policy, visibility into risk, or any staff training. That gap creates real exposure.

We help Maine organizations get ahead of it with practical training, clear governance, and ongoing advisory that scales with your needs.

Common Questions

What most organizations ask first.

Who actually needs an AI audit?

Any organization where staff are using AI tools — even informally — benefits from an audit. If you have no policy, no visibility into how AI is being used, or if you're concerned about data security, an audit is the right first step.

What does AI training actually cover?

Training is tailored to your organization and can cover ChatGPT training, Microsoft Copilot training, prompt writing, AI tool literacy, responsible use expectations, data privacy basics, and process improvement ideas. We build the agenda around what your team actually needs.

How long does each service take?

A training session can be as short as a half-day or span multiple sessions. An audit typically takes 2–4 weeks from discovery to report. Ongoing support is structured as a monthly or quarterly engagement.

Do we have to start with training?

No. Some organizations start with an audit to understand where they are, then follow with training. Others jump straight into ongoing support after an initial engagement. We meet you where you are.

Can we bundle services?

Yes — Training + Audit packages are available at a combined rate. Many organizations start with training and an audit, add AI policy and governance support, then move into ongoing support once they have a baseline established.

Is this only for tech companies?

Not at all. We specifically designed our services for non-tech organizations — small businesses, construction firms, nonprofits, professional services, municipalities, and healthcare-adjacent teams are our primary audience.

Not sure where to start?

Book a free discovery call. We'll help you figure out which service makes sense for your organization — no pressure, no pitch.

Our mission: Make responsible AI education accessible to every Maine organization — regardless of size, sector, or budget. The Maine AI Summit is a free, open event because that is what the mission demands.

What to Expect

A full day of real, practical AI conversation.

The Maine AI Summit is not a tech conference for developers. It is a business conference for the leaders of Maine organizations who are navigating AI adoption right now.

01

Keynotes from AI Experts

Nationally recognized AI researchers giving plain-language talks on what is actually happening at the frontier — and what it means for everyday Maine businesses.

02

Maine Business Panel

Leaders from Maine companies across construction, nonprofits, healthcare-adjacent, and professional services sharing candid, honest accounts of their AI journeys.

03

Interactive Workshop Tracks

Three parallel afternoon workshops: writing your first AI policy, practical prompt engineering for business teams, and an AI risk assessment working session for leaders.

04

AI Governance Deep Dive

A dedicated session on the regulatory landscape, liability questions, and practical policy steps every Maine organization should be taking now.

05

The Future of Work Panel

HR professionals, workforce specialists, and business owners in conversation about how AI is reshaping roles and skills in Maine — and what leaders need to do now.

06

Live Q&A with Every Speaker

Every keynote and panel includes dedicated live Q&A time. Real questions, real answers — no pre-screened softballs.

Conference Topics

The conversations Maine organizations need.

Every session is designed around real business relevance — not theory or technical depth for its own sake.

01

Real-World AI Implementation

How Maine companies across sectors are actually using AI — and what they have learned the hard way.

02

AI Policy & Governance

Practical policy foundations — acceptable-use policies, data standards, and governance that fits an SMB.

03

Cutting-Edge AI Research

What is genuinely happening at the AI frontier — in plain English, without the hype.

04

AI Risk & Data Privacy

The real risks of informal AI adoption — exposure, liability, vendor practices, and mitigation steps.

05

Workforce & Future of Work

How AI is reshaping roles and skills — and what Maine leaders need to do to prepare their teams.

06

Responsible AI Adoption

Building an AI strategy grounded in ethics, transparency, and long-term organizational health.

07

Sector-Specific Insights

Dedicated threads for construction, nonprofits, healthcare-adjacent, professional services, and municipalities.

08

AI Tools in Practice

Hands-on sessions covering prompt engineering, tool selection, and policy drafting you can apply immediately.

09

Building Maine's AI Ecosystem

How Maine's business community and public sector can build a responsible, thriving local AI ecosystem together.

Service 03 — Ongoing AI Support

Turn one-time training into lasting capability.

Retained AI advisory support for Maine organizations that need help with policy updates, tool review, automation questions, and emerging risks.

Scroll
What It Is

Structured advisory support. Not vague consulting.

After training or an audit, most Maine organizations face the same challenge: the work of responsible AI adoption is ongoing. Tools change. Staff turn over. Policies need updating. New risks emerge.

Ongoing Support gives your organization a trusted place to go with real AI questions — on a defined schedule with clear deliverables, not an open-ended retainer that drifts.

  • Monthly or quarterly advisory sessions with a defined agenda
  • AI policy maintenance and updates as tools and regulations evolve
  • Vendor and tool evaluation — new AI software reviewed before adoption
  • Implementation follow-through for initiatives started in training or audit
  • Internal Q&A support for staff AI questions routed through your contact
  • Proactive alerts to emerging AI risks relevant to your industry

Why It's Different

This is advisory support, not open-ended consulting.

Most consulting relationships drift. There's no clear scope, no defined deliverables, and no real accountability for outcomes. You end up paying for access to someone who shows up when asked.

Ongoing Support with AI Impact Maine is structured differently — defined scope, regular cadence, written deliverables every engagement, and a clear understanding of what you're getting and when.

Defined Deliverables

Every session ends with written notes, action items, and clear next steps. No guessing what you received.

Proactive, Not Reactive

We flag emerging AI risks relevant to your industry before they become problems — not just answer questions when you have them.

Mission Accountability

As an, we're structured around long-term local impact — not just billable hours. That shapes how we work.

Easy to Scale

Need more or less engagement as your needs change? Scope adjusts at each renewal — no locked-in contracts.

Common Questions

Questions about Ongoing Support.

Do we need to complete training or an audit first?

No — while Ongoing Support works naturally as a follow-on to training or an audit, we're happy to discuss starting fresh. We'll do a scoping call to understand where you are and design engagement that fits.

What does a typical monthly session look like?

Each session has a pre-shared agenda, covers your open AI questions and policy updates, and ends with written notes and clear action items. Sessions are typically 60–90 minutes via video call, though we can meet onsite for Southern Maine clients.

Can we pause or cancel if our needs change?

Yes. Engagements are structured around renewal periods — monthly or quarterly — and scope adjusts at each renewal. There are no long-term locked-in contracts.

What if we just have occasional questions between sessions?

Both tiers include async email support between sessions. For Monthly Advisory clients, we prioritize response within one business day. You're never left waiting until the next scheduled call for something urgent.

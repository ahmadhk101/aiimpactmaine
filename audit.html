Service 02 — AI Audit for Small Businesses

AI audit for small businesses using AI without a clear plan.

A structured AI governance audit for small businesses and Maine organizations — with a clear report, risk mapping, and a practical responsible AI roadmap.

Scroll
What It Is

See the full picture of AI in your organization.

Most small businesses have more AI exposure than they realize. Staff are using ChatGPT, Copilot, Grammarly, and other tools — often without anyone tracking it, without a policy in place, and without a clear picture of where the risks are.

Our AI Process Audit gives you that picture — clearly documented, honestly assessed, and paired with a realistic roadmap for what to do next. For many Maine small businesses, the audit is the fastest path to practical AI policy consulting, governance priorities, and safer day-to-day use.

What the Audit Covers

  • Current-state AI usage across your organization and workflows
  • AI tool inventory with data handling and risk notes
  • Process mapping — where AI touches operations, decisions, and client data
  • Risk and gap analysis with severity ratings
  • Policy and governance recommendations
  • Prioritized roadmap with clear next steps

The Audit Process

1

Discovery Call

We understand your business, team structure, current AI exposure, and main concerns.

2

Process Review

We map where AI is being used across your operations — formally and informally.

3

Tool Inventory

We catalog all AI tools in use — including ones staff use without formal approval.

4

Risk & Opportunity Mapping

We identify gaps, blind spots, data risks, and areas of positive opportunity.

5

Report & Roadmap

A written report with findings, policy suggestions, and prioritized next actions.

What You Receive

Clear deliverables, not vague recommendations.

01

Current-State Summary

A plain-English overview of how AI is being used across your organization — what tools, what workflows, and what staff are doing with them.

02

Risk & Gap Analysis

A prioritized list of gaps, blind spots, and risks — with severity ratings and context so you know what to address first.

03

Policy Recommendations

Practical governance suggestions and policy starting points — ready to adapt into a formal AI acceptable-use policy for your organization.

04

Priority Roadmap

A sequenced list of recommended next steps — realistic, achievable, and matched to your organization's capacity and priorities.

Ideal For

Organizations already experimenting with AI.

No policy in place

You know staff are using AI tools but have no formal guidance, expectations, or governance around it.

Concerned about data risk

You're worried about what information staff might be putting into AI tools — and whether client or sensitive data is at risk.

Planning a formal rollout

You want to expand AI adoption intentionally and need a clear baseline before investing in training or tools.

New leadership or ownership

You've recently taken over or grown an organization and want to understand the current AI landscape before making decisions.

Compliance considerations

Your industry has data privacy, confidentiality, or regulatory requirements that make unmanaged AI use a real liability.

Board or stakeholder pressure

Funders, board members, or partners have asked about your AI practices and you need a clear, defensible answer.

Common Questions

Questions about the AI Audit.

How long does an AI Process Audit take?

Most audits take 2–4 weeks from discovery call to final report, depending on your organization's size and complexity. Smaller organizations are often completed in under two weeks.

Do we need to have a lot of AI in place already?

No — in fact, many organizations are surprised to discover how much AI is already present in their workflows through everyday tools like Microsoft 365, Grammarly, or customer-facing chatbots. The audit maps what exists, whether formal or informal.

What does the final report include?

You'll receive a plain-English written report covering: a current-state summary of AI use, a risk and gap analysis with severity ratings, policy recommendations, and a prioritized action roadmap. No jargon, no vague suggestions — just clear next steps.

How much does the AI Process Audit cost?

Pricing is scoped during the free discovery call and varies based on organization size. We offer fixed-fee engagements so there are no billing surprises. Grant funding may also be available for qualifying nonprofits and municipalities.

Can the audit be done remotely?

Yes. Most of the audit process is conducted virtually through calls, surveys, and document review. We also offer onsite visits for organizations in Southern Maine where in-person context adds value.

Ready to see where you stand with AI?

An audit starts with a free discovery conversation. No commitment — just clarity.
